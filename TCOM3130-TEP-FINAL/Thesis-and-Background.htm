Technical communication is the process of designing or expressing content that interprets, instructs, or translates information for use by a specific audience.

The public idea of technical forms of writing are focused on the emphasis of scientific knowledge over rhetorical. Design Thinking

Design thinking is a five-step process for solving user challenges by iteratively testing the solution. Anything from a wet floor sign to a traffic light is meant to communicate the same message to not just yourself but the whole population as well. How can we identify the most effective means of communication for an audience greater than 0? User centered design is best for innovating beyond the direct user-interface.

User-Centered Design VS Design Thinking

User-centered design differs from design-thinking in that it is more applied and devoted to the user interface experience. It aims at researching and testing specifics like usability, accessibility, and understandability. Both methods are commonly used throughout Technical Communication since both equally value the customer/audience at the forefront of any solution strategy.
