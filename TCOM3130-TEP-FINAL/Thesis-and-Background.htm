Technical Communication is the process of interpreting information and translating it into a consumable and understandable writing form.

The public idea of technical forms of writing are focused on the emphasis of scientific knowledge over rhetorical. Though many scholars like Bourelle, Bourelle, & Jones have found basis for the coexistence of both in the teaching methods of Technical Communication are useful in developing a multimodal pedagogy. This is one of the subjects that will be covered throughout this literature review as well as the importance of ethics in the Technical Communication field.
